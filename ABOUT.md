# About AIB Portfolio

## At a glance

**AIB Portfolio** is a single-page web application that distils the value proposition of **Microsoft's Business Applications stack** into one navigable, multilingual reference. Its goal is simple: make it easy for anyone — from executive sponsors to architects to end users — to understand what the stack offers, how its pieces fit together, and how it can take an organization further.

---

## Why this site exists

Microsoft's Business Applications portfolio is broad: Dynamics 365, the Power Platform, Copilot Studio, Dataverse, autonomous agents, and dozens of supporting capabilities. Treating it as a flat list of products misses the point — the real value comes from the fact that everything sits on **one unified platform with infinite adaptability**.

This site surfaces that story by decomposing the platform into the elements that matter:

- The **architectural foundation** — a unified, scalable data layer (Dataverse) plus an enterprise-grade security and governance model that the entire stack inherits.
- The **product pillars** — grouped so you can immediately see who each pillar serves and what business outcome it delivers.
- The **AI capabilities** layered across every premium product — agents, copilots, and the latest announcements from Microsoft's Business Applications updates.

The result is a one-stop, no-login reference for the whole portfolio in any of eight languages.

---

## How the content is organized

### 1. Architecture & Foundations

The starting point. Explains the platform's two foundational layers:

- **Dataverse** — the unified, scalable data foundation that every product reads from and writes to, with the **Common Data Model** explained through an interactive visual schema.
- **Enterprise security** — the governance, identity and compliance model that wraps the entire stack.

### 2. Product pillars

Each pillar groups the products that share an audience and an outcome:

- **Customer Experience (Front Office)** — Sales, Customer Service, Customer Insights, Field Service.
- **ERP & Back Office** — Finance, Supply Chain Management, Project Operations, Human Resources, Commerce.
- **SMB Solutions** — Business Central for small and mid-sized organizations.
- **Power Platform** — Copilot Studio, Power Apps, Power Automate, Power Pages.
- **AI Capabilities** — autonomous agents and AI features layered across the stack (see *AI Capabilities* below).

Open any pillar and you see the products it includes. Open a product and you get:

- Its **core and advanced capabilities**
- Its **target audience**
- A short brief on its **architecture and integrations**
- Its **hero licenses** for purchase — official license name, hero **SKU code**, **price**, and a clear explanation of what each tier includes

On every product page you can click the **product name or logo** in the page header to jump directly to that product's **official Microsoft page**.

### 3. AI Capabilities

A dedicated section that focuses on what's new in AI across the portfolio.

- The latest **Microsoft Business Applications Update** keynote video, with **timestamped chapters** so you can jump straight to any specific AI announcement.
- Below the video, a curated catalogue of the latest **AI agents and AI capabilities** shipped inside every premium Dynamics module — each with a link to its **demo video** and to its **official documentation**.

## Multilingual & localized

The site supports eight languages out of the box:

- English (US)
- English (UK)
- Français
- Deutsch
- Español
- Italiano
- العربية (right-to-left)
- Nederlands

Pick a language from the picker in the top header and **all content** — descriptions, capabilities, audience text, license details, and pricing — switches accordingly. **Pricing reflects the official Microsoft list price for the country where each language is primarily spoken**, so users see numbers that are relevant to their market.

Selecting Arabic also flips the entire layout to right-to-left.

Your language choice is remembered between visits.

---

## Light and dark themes

A **light/dark theme toggle** sits in the top header. Your choice is persisted across visits, and dark mode is fully audited for **WCAG color contrast** on every surface — including the language picker dropdown, badges, buttons, hero bands, and the agent cards.

---

## Search

A **search bar** is always available in the top header. It searches across:

- All **categories**
- All **products** (with their parent category)
- All **AI agents and capabilities** linked to those products

Type any keyword and the page switches to a results view; clear the search to return to where you were.

---

## Accessibility

The site is built to **WCAG 2.1 AA**:

- Full keyboard navigation with visible focus rings on every control
- Screen-reader landmarks and unique labels for the desktop sidebar and mobile drawer
- An accessible mobile drawer with focus trap, Escape-to-close, focus restoration on close, and modern `inert` semantics when collapsed
- Respect for the operating system's *Reduce motion* preference
- `<html lang>` and `<html dir>` kept in sync with the active language so screen readers announce text correctly

## Who it's for

- **Executives and decision-makers** who need a clear, jargon-free explanation of how the Microsoft Business Applications stack maps to business outcomes.
- **Architects and pre-sales** who want quick access to architecture briefs, integration notes, license SKUs and pricing in one place.
- **End users and prospects** who want to explore each product, see real AI agents in action, and reach the official Microsoft documentation in two clicks.
- **International teams** who need the same content in their own language, with locally relevant pricing.

---

*AIB Portfolio is a no-login, client-side experience. All content is rendered locally in your browser; nothing about your visit is collected or transmitted.*
