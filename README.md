# About AIB Portfolio

## At a Glance
**AIB Portfolio** is a single-page web application that distills the value proposition of the **Microsoft Business Applications stack** into one navigable, multilingual reference. The goal is simple: empower everyone—from executive sponsors to architects and end users—to understand the stack's offerings, how its components integrate, and how it drives organizational growth.

---

## Why This Site Exists
Microsoft's Business Applications portfolio is expansive, encompassing Dynamics 365, Power Platform, Copilot Studio, Dataverse, autonomous agents, and dozens of supporting capabilities. Viewing these as a simple list of products misses the bigger picture. The true value lies in a **unified platform with infinite adaptability**.

This site brings that story to life by breaking down the platform into its most critical elements:
* **The architectural foundation:** A unified, scalable data layer (Dataverse) and an enterprise-grade security and governance model that the entire stack inherits.
* **The product pillars:** Grouped logically to highlight the target audience and business outcomes for each category.
* **The AI capabilities:** Layered across all premium products, featuring agents, copilots, and the latest updates from Microsoft.

The result is a comprehensive, no-login reference for the entire portfolio, available in eight languages.

---

## How the Content is Organized

### Foundations
The starting point explains the platform's two core pillars:
* **Dataverse:** The unified, scalable data foundation that every product interacts with, featuring a visual explanation of the Common Data Model.
* **Enterprise security:** The governance, identity, and compliance framework wrapping the entire stack.

### Product Pillars
Products are grouped by shared audiences and outcomes:
* **Customer Experience (Front Office):** Sales, Customer Service, Customer Insights, Field Service.
* **ERP & Back Office:** Finance, Supply Chain Management, Project Operations, Human Resources, Commerce.
* **SMB Solutions:** Business Central for small and mid-sized organizations.
* **Power Platform:** Copilot Studio, Power Apps, Power Automate, Power Pages.

### Product Details
Clicking into any specific product reveals comprehensive details:
* **Capabilities:** A breakdown of core and advanced functionalities tailored to specific business needs.
* **Target audience:** Clear definitions of who benefits most from the product.
* **Architecture & integrations:** Briefs detailing how the product fits into the wider ecosystem.
* **Licensing & pricing:** Hero licenses including official names, SKU codes, pricing, and tier breakdowns.
* **Official resources:** Direct, clickable links to the official Microsoft product pages.
* **Guided tours:** Interactive, click-through demos to see the product in action.

### AI Capabilities
A dedicated section focusing on the latest AI innovations across the portfolio:
* **Latest update videos:** The most recent Microsoft Business Applications Update video, featuring timestamped chapters to jump directly to specific AI functionalities.
* **Curated agent directory:** A list of the newest AI agents and capabilities shipped inside premium Dynamics modules, complete with demo videos and official documentation links.

## Multilingual and Localized Experience
The site supports eight languages out of the box:
* English (US)
* English (UK)
* Français
* Deutsch
* Español
* Italiano
* العربية (Right-to-left)
* Nederlands

Selecting a language from the top header updates **all content** instantly—including descriptions, capabilities, licensing, and pricing. Pricing automatically reflects the official Microsoft list price for the primary region of that language, ensuring users see market-relevant figures. Language preferences are automatically saved for future visits.

## Core Features

### Light and Dark Themes
The site includes a persistent **light/dark theme toggle**. Dark mode is fully audited for WCAG color contrast compliance across all surfaces, including dropdowns, badges, buttons, and agent cards.

### Universal Search
A ubiquitous **search bar** is available in the header, allowing users to query:
* Categories and product groupings.
* Individual products and their parent categories.
* Specific AI agents and capabilities.

Typing a keyword switches the view to a dynamic results page; clearing the search instantly returns you to your previous location.

### Accessibility Standards
Built to **WCAG 2.1 AA standards**, the site ensures an inclusive experience with:
* Full keyboard navigation and visible focus rings.
* Screen-reader landmarks and synchronized `<html lang>` and `<html dir>` tags.
* An accessible mobile drawer featuring a focus trap and Escape-to-close functionality.
* Strict adherence to OS-level *Reduce motion* preferences.

## Who It's For
* **Executives and decision-makers:** Seeking clear, jargon-free explanations mapping the stack to business outcomes.
* **Architects and pre-sales:** Requiring quick access to architecture briefs, integration notes, SKUs, and pricing in one centralized hub.

> **Privacy Note:** The AIB Portfolio site is a no-login, purely client-side experience. All content is generated and rendered locally in your browser. No visit data is collected or transmitted.
